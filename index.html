About Me


About Me!

Currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology, Business Analytics, Joel is an individual who enjoys bringing his envisioned ideas come to life through graphic design. As he continues to complete his tertiary education, his main purpose of starting a freelance commission is to learn more, gain experience and hone his craft in developing appealing multimedia designs and websites. His main driver for his motivation comes from his need to support his family to the best he can.

Apart from being a part-time freelancer and student, Joel has taken up responsibilities in coorrdinating social media accounts as well as designing content for them.
